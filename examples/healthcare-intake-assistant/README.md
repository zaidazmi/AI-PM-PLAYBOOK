# Healthcare intake assistant

Synthetic case study showing a high-risk patient-facing workflow where the right product judgment is not to launch AI yet.

## Recommendation

**Prototype only.** Use synthetic data only. Do not pilot with patient data until evals, HIPAA review, security review, observability, incident response, consent, and provider agreements are ready.

## Files

- `opportunity-brief.md`: why the non-AI alternative may be better
- `prd.md`: AI job boundaries, patient workflow, review rules, compliance constraints
- `eval-plan.md`: synthetic scenarios, safety boundaries, escalation tests
- `launch-gate.md`: readiness assessment and recommendation
- `readiness-assessment.yaml`: structured scoring input

Names, dates, metrics, and reviewers are illustrative.
