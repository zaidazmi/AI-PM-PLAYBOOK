# Customer support copilot

Synthetic case study showing a medium-risk AI drafting workflow for Tier 1 support agents.

## Recommendation

**Pilot candidate after blockers.** The problem, workflow, and AI job are strong, but the team must close eval, observability, and low-confidence fallback blockers before starting the pilot.

## Files

- `opportunity-brief.md`: why this problem is worth pursuing
- `prd.md`: AI job, workflow, contracts, quality bar, failure behavior
- `eval-plan.md`: golden examples, rubric, checks, launch thresholds
- `cost-model.md`: unit economics, sensitivity analysis, monitoring triggers
- `launch-gate.md`: readiness assessment and recommendation
- `readiness-assessment.yaml`: structured scoring input

Names, dates, metrics, and reviewers are illustrative.
